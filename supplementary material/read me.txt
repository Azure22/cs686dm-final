Project files are 'data.py', 'naivebayes_lc.py', 'decisiontree_lzl.py', 'util.py' and 'compare.py'. 'test_data.csv', 'test_data_b.csv', 'train_data.csv' and 'train_data_b.csv' are generated by preprocessing. There are some example movie reviews in neg and pos folders. 'imdb.vocab' contains vocabularies and 'imdbEr.txt' contains scores mapping to vocabularies. 'eda.py' is used to analyze the 'test_data.csv' and 'train_data.csv'.
